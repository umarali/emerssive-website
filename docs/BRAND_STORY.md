## The Core Idea

**Problem first. AI/software second.**

Everything hangs off this. Clients don't need AI — they need their business to work better. AI is one of the tools that gets them there. Sometimes it's the right tool. Sometimes it isn't. We tell them which before we start building.

This is what separates Emerssive from every agency that leads with "we do AI" or "we build apps."

## Who We Are

Emerssive is a small, senior-led software development company. We build AI systems, web applications, and mobile products for growing businesses.

**Not a big agency.** You work directly with technical leadership — not account managers, not junior developers who relay your feedback. The people who scope your project are the people who build it.

**Young but proven.** 100+ clients. 1.2M+ monthly users on platforms we've built. 91% of projects delivered on time and on budget.

## Who We Help

Growing teams — typically 1 to 200 people — who face one of three situations:

1. Their team does work that should not be manual
2. They have a product idea but no engineering team
3. Their platform can't keep up with their growth

**Client revenue range:** Pre-revenue to $2.5M+
**Project budget range:** $2,500 (minimum) to $50K+
**First working milestone:** 2–4 weeks from kickoff

We work best with founders, operators, and product owners who know something isn't working — even if they can't name exactly what.

## How We Work

2. **Honest assessment** — we tell you what we can help with and what we can't

## What Makes Us Different

**Problem-first.** We don't start by picking tools or writing code. We start by mapping what's actually slowing the business down — then figure out the simplest thing that fixes it.

**Senior-led.** The engineers who scope your work are the engineers who build it. No layers. No handoffs to a team you've never met.

**Honest.** If we can't help, we say so. If the simplest solution isn't AI, we say so.

**Fast.** Working prototype in 2–4 weeks. We don't run six-week discovery phases that delay your real work.

**Adaptive.** We embed in existing teams, work with existing codebases, and adapt to your setup — not the other way around.

## Case Studies

**Frontline Education**
Problem: Legacy ExtJS platform failing at national K-12 scale.
Built: Modernized frontend + API integration layer.
Result: 1.2M+ monthly visitors, 60% faster, 25% more peak concurrency.

**Alcemi**
Problem: Shopify stores losing buyers before conversion.
Built: AI shopping agent (Claude + Gemini) that surfaces the right product at the right moment.
Result: Personalized recommendations at scale, reduced pre-purchase drop-off.

**Puffbar**
Problem: High-traffic DTC brand needing granular access control across teams.
Built: Role-based management system + analytics dashboards.
Result: Operations teams gained control without touching core infrastructure.

**Qodex.ai**
Problem: API testing slow, manual, and reactive — engineers spending hours on it.
Built: AI-powered API testing and security platform.
Result: 78K+ APIs secured. What took hours became automated.
